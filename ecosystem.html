For Faculty

Faculty Development Programs

Our FDPs are not generic seminars. They are structured engineering development sessions that equip faculty members with the tools, frameworks, and hands-on experience needed to teach cutting-edge technology — and embed a builder's mindset into their classrooms.

Conducted through formal MoU partnerships with colleges and universities, each FDP is customized around the institution's curriculum, student profile, and technology focus areas.

✦ Delivered through MoU-based institutional partnerships
✦ Focus on engineering mindset, not just subject knowledge
✦ Hands-on labs and real technology workshops
✦ Customized for CS, IT, ECE, and allied engineering branches

For Students

Student Development Programs

SDPs are deep engineering experiences for students pursuing B.E, BCA, MCA, Diploma, and allied technical programs. These are not awareness sessions — they are structured development tracks that move students from classroom theory into real technology building.

Students gain exposure to live projects, professional environments, and cross-disciplinary collaboration — all aligned with university requirements and designed for real-world relevance.

✦ Aligned with university and board requirements
✦ Live project exposure with industry mentors
✦ Tracks: IIoT, HVAC, Cybersecurity, Cloud, Web Dev, DevOps, AI/ML
✦ Certificate of completion and recommendation

Competitive Building

Hackathons

Hackathons are our highest-intensity ecosystem events. Multi-team, cross-institutional, time-bound engineering challenges where students don't just ideate — they build. Real prototypes, real code, real systems under pressure.

Every hackathon is aligned to a real problem domain — from infrastructure security to AI-driven systems — ensuring the work produced has genuine relevance beyond the event itself.

✦ 24–48 hour intensive build events
✦ Cross-institutional participation encouraged
✦ Problem domains: Security, Infrastructure, AI, EdTech
✦ Best projects enter the Pragyan Innovation Pipeline

Build & Experiment

Innovation Labs

Innovation Labs are Pragyan EduSec's flagship physical and virtual environments where students move beyond learning and begin building systems. Equipped with tools, structured by engineering methodology, and facilitated by mentors, these labs are where ideas become prototypes.

Labs operate as an ongoing program — not a one-time event. Students return, iterate, and build together over weeks and months, creating a genuine culture of engineering experimentation within their institution.

✦ Ongoing lab model — not a one-time event
✦ Structured by engineering methodology and design thinking
✦ Prototype development, iteration, and documentation
