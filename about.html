Our Story

A Technology Ecosystem
Built for Builders

We realized that while students were studying engineering, they weren't building systems. Most educational frameworks focused on certificates and placements rather than hands-on problem solving.

Pragyan EduSec was founded to bridge this gap. We don't run coaching classes. We build comprehensive ecosystems — engineering programs, research environments, and innovation labs — that transform how students engage with technology across Indian institutions.

01

We Engineer Systems

Every program we build is a system — designed with intent, structured for scale, and built to produce real outcomes. We think like engineers, not educators.

02

We Drive Research

Knowledge without inquiry goes stale. We create environments where students, faculty, and institutions actively research, publish, and push the boundaries of what's possible.

03

We Foster Innovation

From prototype to product — we support ideas at every stage. Our ecosystem gives builders the space, tools, and community to turn raw innovation into real-world impact.

Our Journey

The Pragyan Story

Early builders of a technology ecosystem — from a single idea in Hubballi to institutions across India.

2024 — The Spark

The Ecosystem Idea Was Born

A founding team from Hubballi saw that Indian institutions were producing graduates who could study technology — but not build it. Pragyan EduSec was incorporated to change that.

2025 — Building the Foundation

First Programs & Institutional Partnerships

Launched pilot Faculty Development Programs (FDPs), Student Development Programs (SDPs), and signed the first MOU-based partnerships with colleges across Karnataka.

2026 — Scaling Activity

15+ Institutions. 500+ Engineers Trained.

Expanded programs to 15+ institutions, conducted hackathons, research presentations, and innovation workshops — all while developing our internal technology infrastructure.

2028 — The Full Ecosystem

India's Technology Ecosystem, Complete

Our target: a thriving, interconnected ecosystem of students, researchers, labs, products, and institutions — all contributing to India-first technology innovation at scale.
